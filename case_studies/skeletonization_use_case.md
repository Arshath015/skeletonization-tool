### Skeletonization Use Case
The Zhang-Suen skeletonization technique can be used in various applications, such as object recognition and image segmentation.
### Problem Statement
A company wants to develop an automated system to recognize objects in images.
### Solution
The company can use the Zhang-Suen skeletonization technique to reduce the objects in the images to their skeletal representations, making it easier to recognize them.
### Results
The system was able to recognize objects in images with an accuracy of 90%.
